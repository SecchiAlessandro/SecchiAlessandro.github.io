Experience

Control Software Engineer

Hitachi Energy

I am a power electronics engineer at Hitachi Energy's Global Center of Competence in Turgi, Switzerland. I am working for the business line PGGI, GPQS that means I deal with medium power converters for grid integration projects (Rail, distribution grids, mines, offshore platforms..). In the past two years I have been working mainly with Static Synchronous Compensator (STATCOM), from its software development and HIL testing to the site commissioning.
It's a cool job that allows me to take many resposabilities.
Sometimes, I see our unit as a start-up with a hundred years of experience. We are about 15 control software engineers and we are quite lucky cause we are involved in the whole design of these medium-power converters, including tender support, electrical design, software development and testing, on-site commissioning, and service. But the most interesting part is that, our solutions (NPC PCS6000 and MMC) are evolving projects following market and customer requirements. So, we are actually part of this fantastic Energy Transition! üåçüåø

So far, I have worked on different projects:

  • Anglogld STATCOM, which will contribute to the electrification of the Geita Gold Mine in Tanzania, Africa.
  • WPD Western Power Distribution STATCOM, UK.
  • Draugen STATCOM for offshore platform, Norway.
  • Other Static Frequency Converters (SFC) for Rail projects.

The software that we commonly used are: Matlab/Simulink, Compact Control Builder (CCB), Python (automated framework), Xviewer Yokogawa..
